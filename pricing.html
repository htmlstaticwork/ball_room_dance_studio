Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior dance experience to join? +

Not at all. Our Trial Session is specifically designed for complete beginners. Your instructor will assess your natural rhythm, coordination, and goals before recommending the right style and package for you.

How long does each private lesson last? +

All private lessons are 45 minutes long. This is the industry standard duration that allows for focused technique work, practice, and instructor feedback without fatigue. Group classes and social events run for 60–90 minutes.

Can I switch packages or upgrade later? +

Yes. You can upgrade to a higher package at any time and the remaining value of your current package will be credited toward the new one. Simply speak to your instructor or contact us directly to arrange the transition.
